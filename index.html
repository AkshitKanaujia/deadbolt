Deadbolt

Deadbolt is an iPhone app blocker. Set the dial, tap Bolt it, and the apps that eat your day stay locked until time's up.

Support

Questions, problems, or ideas? Email y19iitk@gmail.com — a human reads every message.

Common questions

  • Blocking isn't working? Make sure Deadbolt has Screen Time access: iOS Settings → Screen Time → Apps with Screen Time Access.
  • Stuck in a strict session? That's the feature working — it ends automatically at the time you chose. Need out early? Request an emergency exit in the app and wait out the 10-minute cooldown.
  • Manage or cancel your subscription: iOS Settings → your name → Subscriptions.
