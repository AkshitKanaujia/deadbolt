Common questions

  • Blocking isn't working? Make sure Deadbolt has Screen Time access: iOS Settings → Screen Time → Apps with Screen Time Access.
  • Stuck in a strict session? That's the feature working — it ends automatically at the time you chose. Need out early? Request an emergency exit and wait out the ten-minute cooldown, or walk 200 steps.
  • Manage or cancel your subscription: iOS Settings → your name → Subscriptions.
